New faces, new places. Orientation week, campus tours, and the beginning of something extraordinary.

Hello World felt like launching a rocket. We wrote our first lines of code — and broke everything beautifully.
Year 2 · 2016
First Major courses
Group projects, sleepless nights, and the sweet taste of a working application on demo day.
Year 3 · 2023
survival
72 hours, zero sleep, infinite caffeine. We competed, we built, and we even won.

Research proposals, literature reviews, supervisor meetings. The most challenging chapter.
Year 5· 2018

Real offices, real deadlines, real impact. We brought our university knowledge into the professional world.
Year 5 · 2018

Months of work distilled into a single presentation. We walked in nervous and walked out transformed.

The caps go up. The tears flow freely. And a new chapter — written entirely by us — begins.
